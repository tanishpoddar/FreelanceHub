Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FreelanceHub and how to make the most of your portfolio.

Getting Started

To create your first portfolio, simply register for an account and click "Create Portfolio" in your dashboard. You can then add your personal information, skills, projects, and testimonials using our intuitive drag-and-drop editor.

Yes! FreelanceHub is completely free to use. You can create and manage your portfolio, receive client inquiries, and access all basic features without any cost.

You can create a basic portfolio in just 10-15 minutes. For a comprehensive portfolio with multiple projects and testimonials, we recommend spending 30-60 minutes to showcase your work effectively.

Portfolio Management

Yes, you can set your portfolio to private mode. Private portfolios are only visible to you and won't appear in search results or browse pages. You can toggle this setting in your portfolio dashboard.

In your portfolio editor, click "Add Project" and fill in the project details including title, description, technologies used, and project links. You can also upload images to showcase your work visually.

Currently, we offer a clean, professional design that works well for all types of freelancers. We're working on adding more customization options in future updates.

Client Communication

Clients can contact you through the contact form on your portfolio page. You'll receive email notifications for all inquiries, and you can manage them in your dashboard.

No, we don't charge any commission on your projects. FreelanceHub is a portfolio platform that helps you showcase your work and connect with clients. All project negotiations and payments are handled directly between you and your clients.

When you receive an inquiry, you'll get an email notification with the client's contact information. You can then respond directly to their email to discuss project details, timelines, and pricing.

Analytics & Insights

We provide portfolio view counts, visitor analytics, and inquiry tracking. You can see how many people visit your portfolio, where they come from, and how many inquiries you receive.

Analytics are updated in real-time. View counts and visitor data are recorded immediately when someone visits your portfolio.

Technical Support

FreelanceHub works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend using the latest version of your browser for the best experience.

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link via email.

You can contact our support team through the contact form on our website or email us directly at support@freelancehub.com. We typically respond within 24 hours.
